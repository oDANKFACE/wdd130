Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to expand our business from a local home-town/billboard tourist attraction, and give it an online presence that modernizes our services. Booking tours, getting directions, meeting the crew, reading reviews, everything directed toward increasing our revenue and giving us a foothold in a space that has incredible advertising potential.

Audience

Our customers are primarily middle class young adult outdoorsy types, and families seeking outdoor activities/tourist attractions. Their interests may include hiking, photography, rock climbing, thrill seeking, nature, and/or water sports. Their needs include medium to large group activities, activities that are proctored by another party, and getting in touch with nature.
